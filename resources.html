UK Mental Health & Neurodivergent Resources

Explore trusted organisations and services supporting mental health and neurodivergent communities.

Emergency & Crisis Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or having thoughts of self-harm, get help now.

  • 999 – Emergency services for immediate life-threatening situations.
  • Samaritans (116 123) – 24/7 confidential support for anyone in emotional distress.
  • Crisis Text Line – Text SHOUT to 85258 for 24/7 crisis support via text.
  • NHS Crisis Lines – Local mental health crisis helplines in your area.
  • Mind Crisis Services – Information about crisis support services.
  • PAPYRUS (0800 068 4141) – Suicide prevention support for young people under 35.

General Mental Health Support

  • Mind – Advice and support for anyone experiencing mental health issues.
  • YoungMinds – Support and resources for young people's mental health.
  • NHS Mental Health Services – Official NHS support for mental health care.
  • Rethink Mental Illness – Support for people affected by mental illness.
  • Mental Health Foundation – Resources for improving mental health and wellbeing.

Neurodivergent Support

  • National Autistic Society – Leading UK charity for autistic people and their families.
  • ADHD Foundation – Neurodiversity charity supporting ADHD and related conditions.
  • ADHD UK – Support and information for people with ADHD.
  • Dyslexia Action – Support for people with dyslexia and learning differences.

Specialist Support

  • British Association of Art Therapists – Resources on therapeutic art for mental health.
  • BACP (British Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy) – Find qualified therapists and counsellors.
  • Relate – Relationship counselling and support services.
  • Cruse Bereavement Care – Support for people experiencing grief and bereavement.
